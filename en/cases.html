Hua & Hua Million-Yuan Creativity Awards

For Every Value We Champion, There Is an Award

Since the first Hua & Hua Million-Yuan Creativity Awards in 2014—and the sixth edition's first public presentation in 2019—the competition has been held every December to reward Super Cases that create lasting impact for companies and even entire industries. First prize is RMB 1 million, second prize RMB 300,000 and third prize RMB 200,000. A million-yuan idea must meet three standards: put the enterprise first, demonstrate clear cause and effect, and advance the industry.
