Frequently Asked Questions

Q1

We need brand strategy, positioning or a brand upgrade. What can Hua & Hua solve?

We do more than refresh visual identity. We help a company define the position its brand should occupy, the words and signs by which consumers will remember it, and then align brand strategy, product portfolio, reasons to buy and communication assets.

Q2

We need marketing, awareness, channel recruitment and customer acquisition. Is Hua & Hua an advertising agency?

Hua & Hua performs the role of an advertising agency, but also combines product development and strategic consultancy. We design product, strategy, channels and communication together rather than delivering a single advertising idea or media placement.

Q3

We are launching a new product or want to build a hero product. Can Hua & Hua work from the product side?

Yes. We work across product strategy, new-product planning, naming, packaging, reasons to buy and communication actions, determining whether the new product can become a key engine of growth rather than merely redesigning its packaging.

Q4

Our corporate strategy is unclear, or we are moving from a regional to a national brand. Is Hua & Hua a good fit?

Potentially—provided the owner is involved, the budget is clear and the organisation is willing to execute. Hua & Hua is suited to growth pathways, business transformation, national expansion and full-year, all-round planning.

Q5

We only need packaging, a logo, visual identity, a commercial or design materials. Can we engage Hua & Hua?

Hua & Hua creates packaging, logos, commercials and design materials—the work shown in our cases is produced by our own teams. However, these services are available only within our full-year, all-round engagement model. We do not accept stand-alone design projects.
