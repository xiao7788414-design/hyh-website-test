Mr. Sam Hua was invited to give an English speech at the National University of Singapore.

On the evening of May 24, 2023, Mr. Sam Hua presented "The Basic Logic of Art of War's 13 Chapters," fully in English, at the National University of Singapore. This time, Mr. Sam also reflected on Hua & Hua's corporate spirit of working hard. In order for the English-speaking Singaporeans to understand "Sun Tzu's Art Of War,' Mr. Sam spent almost 2 months translating his speech from Mandarin to English and practicing his English speech for two hours every morning at nine, accompanied by his English teacher. Not only were his efforts tremendous, but he has truly achieved the goal of enabling the world to understand "Sun Tzu's Art of War."
