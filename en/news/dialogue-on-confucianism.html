Mr. Sam Hua was invited as a guest speaker by the renowned Singaporean cultural scholar, Ms. Wang Hongyu

On 1st February 2024, Mr. Sam Hua was invited as a guest speaker by the renowned Singaporean cultural scholar, Ms. Wang Hongyu. At the Singapore Chinese Cultural Centre's sixth-floor library, they engaged in a spiritual dialogue on Confucianism, discussing "The Great Learning" and "The Doctrine of the Mean." In the final segment of the dialogue, Mr. Sam Hua presented two sets of series books to the Chinese Cultural Centre, and Ms. Kong Yuan, Deputy Director of the Chinese Cultural Centre, reciprocated with the Centre's mascot for the Year of the Dragon. This dialogue was simultaneously live-streamed on five platforms: the domestic "Hua & Hua Business School" Douyin account, "Hua & Hua" video account, "Hua Shan 2009", and the overseas "Sam Hua" Facebook and "Sam Hua" Youtube channels, attracting over 52,000 viewers.
